1st Layer: Edge of the Abyss

Located beneath the town of Orth, it is a place where all whistle ranks can safely traverse as there are typically no dangerous creatures that inhabit the region. The edge of the abyss is mostly serene and familiar as the biome is home to rivers and lush vegetation.

INFORMATION

DEPTH: 0 ~ 1,350 meters
CURSE: Mild dizziness and nausea
REQUIRED RANK: Red Whistle

INHABITANTS
  • Demonfish
  • Hammerbeak
  • Sakawatari (Hunting)
  • Silkfang
  • Unknown Creatures
